High Cube Containers

Do you need to high cube container?

Box Bulk Containers Ltd, sell new and good quality used 20ft and 40ft high cube containers, delivered and offloaded to your premises throughout Australia. High Cube containers have an additional height of 1ft making the total height of the high cube container 9ft 6inches. This extra height allows additional storage space and ventilation for the interior of the container.

20ft new / used High Cube Containers

  • Guaranteed wind and watertight eg fully weather proof
  • Suitable for all type of storage
  • Highly secure and suitable for all applications

  • Ventilated to aid in the reduction of condensation
