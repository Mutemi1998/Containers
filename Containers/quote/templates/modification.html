Container Modification

Do you need a bespoke container conversion?

Here at Box Bulk Containers Ltd, we offer container conversion services whereby we can convert a container to your requirements. Container conversions are great for creating, for example, container offices, toilets, homes, bike stores, sport changing rooms, market stalls and many more bespoke conversion types.

Container conversions are ideal for generating a bespoke container type and utilising the containers space to create a unique conversion. We offer a variety of container conversion types and you can also request a bespoke conversion that we can look at - to see if we can convert it for you.

about our container conversions

  • We offer container conversion services in Australia at one of our container depots (please contact us to learn more about our conversion services).
  • Select from a variety of container conversion types including market stall conversions, locker room conversions, home conversions, office conversions, bespoke conversions and many more.
  • Conversions are great for being creative with your shipping container and utilising the space and resources to build your ideal conversion.
  • Send us a request for a completely bespoke container conversion, such as a sketch or a diagram, and we can discuss your requirements with you.
