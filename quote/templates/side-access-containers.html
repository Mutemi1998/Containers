Side Access Containers

Do you need a side access container?

Side Access Containers (also known as full side opening containers) available new & used, in 20ft and 40ft sizes.

Side Access containers are classed as ‘special build’ and are not as easily available to purchase as standard containers.  They all command a much higher outright purchase price.  This leads to a shortage of used containers of this type, therefore please bear this in mind when you are looking to purchase a side door/side access container from Box Bulk Containers Ltd.

Side Access Container Features

  • 20ft and 40ft in length
  • 8ft wide and 8ft 6 in height
  • Side access containers are normally sold as new one way shipped containers – they are less available in used condition
  • Suitable for shipment with valid CSC plate
  • Suitable for static storage within Australia
  • Ideal for limited access with so many door opening options
  • Door configure in several options for easy loading
  • Normally offered in colour is Blue, green or brown
  • Every door is fitted with lock-box facilities ensuring total security
  • Suitable for conversion to market stalls, temporary or secure mobile shops and the like
