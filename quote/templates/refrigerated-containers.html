Refrigerated Containers

Do you need a Temperature Controlled / Refrigerated, Cold Storage Container?

Here at Box Bulk Containers, we have a large range of refrigerated storage containers available to buy or hire.

Refrigerated containers, sometimes referred to as reefer containers or portable cold store units can store goods at a temperature range of between +25 to -25. We offer a variety of sizes of refrigerated containers to suit every space requirement including 10ft, 20ft, 30ft & 40ft.

Refrigerated Container hire

When hiring a refrigerated container, we offer a unique, exclusive 24-hour emergency breakdown service on the machinery section. Our call-out service is manned by our own staff who know you and your business, you will never be put through to a third party call centre. We have engineers based throughout Australia mainland.

Our refrigerated containers for hire are supplied as standard with lightweight aluminium container ramps suitable for handballing or roll cages or non-motorised pallet trucks.
